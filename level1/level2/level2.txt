The DevOps taxonomy was refined and made publicly available in 2021 through the release of a white paper titled, "The DevOps: A Concise Understanding to the DevOps Philosophy and Science".[9] The DevOps taxonomy consists of Philosophy and Science, their respective branches (Epistemology and Ontology; Social and Applied) and sub-domains (Belief, Knowledge, and Truth; Sociology, Psychology, Economics, and Engineering) with provided instantiations (Agile, Quality Assurance, Lean; Culture Transformation, Faster Time to Market, and Automation).
